Daily Declarations
Speak It Daily
  • W1
    I affirm that I am in God's place for my life! Every day of my life, I experience an overflow of financial blessings, wealth, and prosperity, all to the glory of God.

    My life is a manifestation of His abundance and grace, and I am consistently making progress in every area of my life. In Christ, I have access to the unshakable riches of God. I'm empowered to succeed, and every investment I make multiplies exponentially.

    The law of multiplication is at work in my finances — everything I touch flourishes. Goodness and mercy surround my wealth, and my prosperity knows no end. God has granted me unlimited resources, and I am connected to a limitless supply. My wealth grows effortlessly, and I am divinely positioned for financial success. Nothing in my hands diminishes, for I am a steward of God's inexhaustible abundance. Hallelujah! Glory to God!
  • W2
    I am blessed beyond measure — the Lord has lavished His goodness upon me, and I walk in the fullness of His divine provision. God's blessing has made me rich, and there's no sorrow in my path. I am empowered to create wealth, and my finances overflow with supernatural increase.

    I am blessed with great riches and wealth. The angels of wealth and riches are in my house, and my righteousness is established forever. I walk in supernatural prosperity; my ways are past finding out. The blessing of the Lord has permanently rested upon me. I am swimming in an overflow — an abundance of wealth and fortune. The Lord has crowned my year with goodness, and my paths drip with abundance.
  • W3
    God has brought me into a large place; I flourish like the palm tree, growing mightily like the cedar of Lebanon. Everything I get involved with prospers and yields profits beyond natural comprehension.

    I am diligent in all my dealings, and this has caused me to stand before kings. I live in unexplainable financial abundance. The glory, grace, wisdom, and perfections of God are fully expressed through my finances. Hallelujah!

    God has unveiled and revealed to me doors of prosperity which no eye has seen, no ear has heard, and which have not entered the heart of men. I am not part of the suffering masses. I am made for life and set up for honour, on the path of supernatural success!
