Why Join
Building a Rock Solid Foundation in Christ

It's more than just a class—it's a journey to discover your purpose, strengthen your walk with God, and become Rock Solid in your faith. You'll leave equipped, confident, and ready to make an impact wherever you go!

Course Overview
01

New Creation

Discover who you truly are in Christ and the powerful life He's called you to live.

02

The Holy Spirit

Learn how to walk closely with the Holy Spirit and live a Spirit-led life every day.

03

Christian Doctrines

Understand the core truths of God's Word that keep your faith strong and unshakable.

04

Evangelism & Cell Ministry

Share your faith confidently and make real impact through your cell group.

05

Christian Character

Build the right habits, attitude, and lifestyle that reflect the nature of Christ in you.

06

Local Assembly & Loveworld

Learn how the local church functions within the larger Loveworld nation.

07

Technology for Growth

Use digital tools, platforms, and media to strengthen your spiritual life.

The Experience

🎉 Graduation Ceremony

We end with a special graduation ceremony to celebrate your commitment and growth in the Word. You'll leave equipped, confident, and ready to make an impact wherever you go!
